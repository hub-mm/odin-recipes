Spaghetti Carbonara

Finished Spaghetti Carbonara

This recipe will show you how to create a quick and easy carbonarra. The process should take no longer than 15mins and leave you with a tasty ready to go italian dish to add to your repetoire.

    Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Guanciale
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

    Steps

  1. Get water boiling.
  2. Put olive oil in pan and let sit on low heat.
  3. Cut up garlic and place in pan with guanciale, stir throughout.
  4. Let cook on a low heat.
  5. Put spaghetti in boiling water - set 10min timer.
  6. Whils pasta is cooking place three eggs in a bowl.
  7. Add 150g of parmesan to egg bowl, whisk until thoroughly mixed.
  8. Once the pasta is ready, drain the water. However, aim to leave a couple table spoons behind.
  9. Put spaghetti into guanciale and garlic pan and stir while adding the remaning pasta water ontop.
  10. Remove pan from heat and add the egg and parmesan mix to the pan.
  11. Stir until fully mixed.
  12. Dish up and enjoy! Your food is ready.
