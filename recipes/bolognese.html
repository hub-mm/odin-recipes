Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Recipe passed on from a traditional Italian grandfather.

The ultimate comfort food. Leftovers freeze nicely!

To make this recipe more authentic, you can use a mixture of ground veal, pork, and beef.

You can also substitute the balsamic vinegar for 1/2 cup of red wine.

Prep Time

20 minutes

Cook Time

40 minutes

Total Time

60 minutes

Dished up Spaghetti Bolognese

    Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 slices bacon, diced
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 (28 ounce) cans crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • Â¼ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

    Instructions:

  1. Prepare Pasta
    • Cook spaghetti for about 10 mintues in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook Bacon and Vegetables
    • In a large skillet or saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced bacon and cook until slightly crisp.
    • Add the finely chopped onion, celery, and carrot. Cook until the vegetables are soft and the onions are translucent.
  3. Add Seasoning and Beef
    • Stir in the minced garlic and dried oregano, cooking for about 1 minute until fragrant.
    • Add the ground beef, breaking it up with a spoon. Cook until browned and no longer pink.
  4. Deglaze and add Tomatoes
    • Pour in the balsamic vinegar to deglaze the pan, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom.
    • Stir in the crushed tomatoes and tomato paste. Mix well.
  5. Simer the sauce
    • Add the white sugar, salt, and black pepper. Reduce the heat and let the sauce simmer for about 30 minutes to allow flavors to meld together.
  6. Finish the Sauce
    • Just before serving, stir in the chopped fresh basil.
  7. Serve
    • Toss the cooked spaghetti with the sauce. Serve hot, topped with freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
  8. Enjoy your meal!
