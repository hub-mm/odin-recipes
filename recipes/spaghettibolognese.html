Spaghetti Bolognese

Finished Spaghetti Bolognese

This recipe will show you how to create a quick and easy bolognese. The process should take no longer than 30mins but can however, theoretically take as long as you want. Leaving you with a tasty ready to go italian dish to add to your repetoire.

    Ingredients
  • Spaghetti
  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Tomatos
  • Beef stock cube
  • Tomatop paste
  • Oarmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
    Steps
  1. Put oil in pan.
  2. Cut up onion and garlic (quantity to your discretion) and put in pan on low heat.
  3. Cut up tomatos and place in pan with onion and garlic, stir pans content.
  4. Heat up a seperate pan and add a small amount of oil.
  5. Place ground beef into newly heated up pan.
  6. Break up and stir beef until brown.
  7. Once brown, add beef to tomato, onion and garlic pan.
  8. Stir until fully mixed and let cook on a low heat.
  9. Add tomato paste, beef stock cube and basil.
  10. Stir pans contents until well mixed.
  11. Get water boiling. Place spaghetti in boiling water and set a 10mins timer.
  12. Occasionally stir pan containing beef throughout. Once pasta is ready drain.
  13. Dish up and enjoy! Your food is ready to go.
